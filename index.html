Our Story

Welcome to Pizza Universe, where every slice is a journey through flavor and fun. Founded in 1988 by a group of pizza obsessed best friends, we set out to craft the most vibrant, bold, and delicious pizzas the galaxy has ever tasted. We use only the freshest of ingredients, handmade dough and sauces from scratch daily.

Our dough is slow fermented for 48 hours, giving it the perfect and chewy crust that keeps the customers wanting more! Every pizza is baked in our stone deck oven at 450 degrees.

We source our tomatoes directly from farms based in Southern Italy, and our mozzarella is delivered fresh every morning, No reuses, no left overs.

Our team of passionate chefs brings creativity and energy to every slice of pie. From classic Margherita to our signature pie The Galaxy Supreme, there is something for everyone.
